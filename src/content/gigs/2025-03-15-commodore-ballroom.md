# Album Release Show at The Commodore Ballroom

Join us for an unforgettable evening celebrating the release of our debut album *Surf Sessions* at Vancouver's legendary Commodore Ballroom.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Saturday, March 15th, 2025
- **Doors:** 8:00 PM
- **Show:** 9:00 PM
- **Venue:** The Commodore Ballroom
- **Address:** 868 Granville Street, Vancouver, BC

## What to Expect

We'll be performing all tracks from *Surf Sessions* plus some brand new material we've been working on. Expect a high-energy set featuring our signature reverb-drenched guitar instrumentals that blend surf rock traditions with modern production techniques.

## Special Guests

Opening the night will be local favorites The Beachcombers, a surf rock quartet that shares our love for instrumental music and West Coast vibes.

Tickets are available through Ticketmaster and at the door. Early bird pricing available until March 1st.

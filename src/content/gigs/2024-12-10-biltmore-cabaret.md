# Winter Surf Night at The Biltmore Cabaret

What a night! We had an incredible time at The Biltmore Cabaret for our Winter Surf Night performance.

## Setlist

1. **Breaking Waves** (Original)
2. **Pipeline** (The Chantays cover)
3. **Pacific Highway** (Original)
4. **Misirlou** (Dick Dale cover)
5. **Storm Surge** (Original)
6. **Walk Don't Run** (The Ventures cover)
7. **Beach Blanket** (Original)
8. **Apache** (The Shadows cover)
9. **Tidal Pool** (Original)
10. **Wipe Out** (The Surfaris cover)

## The Show

The intimate setting of The Biltmore Cabaret was perfect for our sound. The venue's acoustics really brought out the reverb and echo effects that define our style. We played to a sold-out crowd of surf rock enthusiasts who were incredibly supportive throughout the night.

## Highlights

- Performing our original track "Storm Surge" for the first time live
- The crowd's enthusiastic response to our rendition of "Pipeline"
- Meeting so many new fans after the show

Thanks to everyone who came out and made this such a memorable night! Special thanks to The Biltmore Cabaret for having us.

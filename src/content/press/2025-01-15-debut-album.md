# Orange Whip Releases Debut Album 'Surf Sessions'

**Vancouver, BC** â€” Local instrumental rock trio Orange Whip has announced the release of their debut album, *Surf Sessions*, available now on all major streaming platforms.

The album features 12 tracks of reverb-drenched guitar instrumentals that blend surf rock traditions with modern production techniques. Recorded at Vancouver's historic Warehouse Studio, the album captures the band's signature sound that has been electrifying local venues for the past two years.

# Orange Whip Featured on CBC Radio's 'The Early Edition'

**Vancouver, BC** — Orange Whip made their radio debut this morning on CBC Radio One's *The Early Edition*, performing live in studio and discussing their unique approach to instrumental surf rock.

The band performed an acoustic version of their hit track "Pacific Highway" and talked with host Stephen Quinn about their creative process, Vancouver's music scene, and the inspiration behind their debut album *Surf Sessions*.

## What the Critics Are Saying

> "Orange Whip brings a fresh energy to the surf rock genre while staying true to its roots. Their instrumental compositions are both nostalgic and contemporary." — *Vancouver Sun*

> "The band's ability to tell stories without words is remarkable. Each track on *Surf Sessions* takes you on a journey." — *Georgia Straight*

The interview will be available for replay on CBC's website for the next 30 days. Orange Whip's next performance is scheduled for March 15th at The Commodore Ballroom.
